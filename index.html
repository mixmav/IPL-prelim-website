The Indian Premier League (IPL) is a professional Twenty20 cricket league in India contested every year by franchise teams representing Indian cities. The league, founded by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) member Lalit Modi in 2007, is scheduled over April and May of every year The title sponsor of the IPL in 2016 is Vivo Electronics, thus the league is officially known as the Vivo Indian Premier League. The IPL is the most-attended cricket league in the world and ranks sixth among all sports leagues.

Shirin Sadikot An essayist and journalist by profession, Shirin Hasan Sadikot is a Mass Communications major with a focus on sports writing and comparative literature. Interested in Cricket, thoughts come out of her regular reading habits and deepest intellectual concerns on the modern day sport. She writes to illustrate and to an extent define the times in which we live. She is a black and white person. She doesn’t see greys. Her strength lies in the field of non-fiction writing. She does a lot of travelling and wanders the world with a pen and notebook. Off-late just laptop. A seasoned journalist with nearly 7 years of experience in covering Cricket, she has built an extensive network of friends across different cricketing nations, which has enabled her to do in-depth research and deliver quality articles. Her style is both simple and declarative, phrases and sentences that pop and push off one another, creating their own energy. While she continues to read and research about the sport, through her writings she aims to scribble down the ups and downs of the great sport – Cricket.
